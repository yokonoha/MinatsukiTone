##  About the Concept and Name: MinatsukiTone  

The name **"Minatsuki"** is a variation on the Japanese word **"Minazuki" (æ°´ç„¡æœˆ)**, which traditionally refers to **June**, the sixth month of the Japanese lunar calendar.  

The visual concept is derived directly from the kanji that form the word:  

* **æ°´ (Mizu)**: **Water**  
* **ç„¡ (Na)**: A connecting particle (This character literally means "nothing" or "without," but in this specific context, it is interpreted as a particle connecting "Water" and "Month".)   
* **æœˆ (Tsuki)**: **Month/Moon**  

---

### Relationship to the App's UI Colors  

These kanji are the foundation of my design's colors:  

1.  **Water (æ°´):** This character is represented by **Mizuiro (æ°´è‰²), or "Water Blue,"** which serves as the bright, primary theme color for the UI.
2.  **Moon (æœˆ):** This connection inspires the use of a **deep, subdued black** (or "Night Shade"). This hue evokes the **night sky gently illuminated by the moonlight**, providing necessary depth and a beautiful contrast to the Water Blue.  

These two core colorsâ€”Water Blue and Night Shade Blackâ€”define the unique visual identity of the MinatsukiTone user interface.
